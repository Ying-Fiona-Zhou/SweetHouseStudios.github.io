Events

We specialize in photographing a wide variety of events — from intimate gatherings to large-scale public shows. Whether it's a cultural festival, a corporate function, or a private celebration, our team captures every meaningful moment with creativity and precision.
Venues we have experience working with include:

  • Traditional Chinese Show
  • Coffee Shop
  • NJ Barn
  • The State Hotel

Pricing is based on the number of guests, the subject of the photos, and the duration of the event. Because these factors vary greatly depending on the type of event, we recommend booking a consultation appointment for an accurate estimate of costs.

A typical small event package resembles the First Bank Holiday Party:

  • A guest list of less than 100
  • A four-hour long ceremony and reception
  • Custom "shot list" of 25+ edited proofs
  • Your choice of 15+ edited proofs
  • 50+ shots covering the event and guests

This package typically begins at $900 but will vary depending on the number of locations, travel, the types of poses, etc. A more accurate estimate is available with a consultation.

A typical large event package resembles the 50th Annual Art Creation Convention:

  • Over 500 attendees
  • A five-hour long event
  • Custom "shot list" of 15+ edited proofs
  • 35+ shots of maximum 35 key guests during the event
  • Your choice of 15+ edited proofs
  • 150+ shots covering the event and guests

This package typically begins at $2,400 but will vary depending on the number of locations, travel, the types of poses, etc. A more accurate estimate is available with a consultation.

Not sure what a "shot list" is or what "key guests" means? Check out our FAQ page!
