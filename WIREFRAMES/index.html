Welcome!

Let us capture your precious moments.

Sweet House Studio is a portrait, event, and commercial photography studio based in Metuchen, NJ. We offer a wide range of services, including wedding, maternity, and graduation photography. Since 2016, Sweet House Studio has proudly specialized in family photography, helping clients capture timeless moments they will cherish for a lifetime. We invite you to explore our website and reach out with any questions or comments — we look forward to connecting with you and capturing your beautiful memories!
