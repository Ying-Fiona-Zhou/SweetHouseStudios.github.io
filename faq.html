Frequently Asked Questions

How can I get accurate pricing for my session?

We provide quotes after a quick consultation so coverage time, location, and deliverables match your needs.

What is a "shot list"?

A short list of must-have people and moments. We’ll help you keep it simple and realistic.

What do you mean by "key guests"?

VIPs you want prioritized—family members, speakers, sponsors, or special attendees.

How does a consultation work?

We review your goals, timeline, location, and style, then recommend a package and send a clear quote.

In-studio vs on-location portraits—what’s the difference?

Studio sessions are private with controlled lighting; on-location sessions feel more candid and environmental.

Do you allow walk-ins?

Sessions are typically by appointment so we can plan the look and deliverables. Message us for availability.
