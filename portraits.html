Portraits

We offer everything from professional solo head shots to natural and candid group photos. Through an in-studio session, we are sure to capture the moments you wish to remember or gift to a loved one.

We provide framed prints, wall canvases, mini photo books, print boxes, coffee table albums, and more. Our full catalog is available in our studio for you to view during your session with us.

Unsure of how many prints you want to order? Because the cost of the photo session and the products are separate, feel free to purchase products following the session.

A typical studio family portrait package resembles the three-member Ayden-Gao family:

  • A private one-hour session in our studio and access to our props
  • A digital copy of your choice of eight edited proofs
  • An 18"x24" framed portrait of the proof of your choice
  • 12 6"x4" printed photographs of up to two proofs of your choice
  • 24 2"x3" printed photographs of up to two proofs of your choice

This package typically begins at $350 but prices may vary; please request a consultation for a more accurate estimate.

To inquire about availability, special requests, and/or a detailed estimate, please request a consultation.
