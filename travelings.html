Travelings

When it comes to weddings—or any meaningful new beginning—there’s nothing more important than having a skilled photographer to capture each precious moment, preserving memories that will be retold for generations.

We understand that wedding planning can be both stressful and expensive. But finding a high-quality photographer shouldn’t add to that burden. That's why we offer a variety of packages at different price points, all designed to deliver beautiful results without breaking the bank.

A typical traveling package includes:

  • Coverage of events with over 120 guests
  • A customized shot list with 25+ fully edited images
  • 20+ edited photos capturing the full ceremony, from processional to recessional
  • 15+ candid and posed shots of the couple during the reception
  • 100+ spontaneous moments of guests enjoying the celebration

This package typically starts at $1,200, with the final price varying based on factors such as number of locations, travel distance, and shot complexity. A detailed estimate is available during a consultation.
Some of the locations we’ve had the pleasure of working in include:

  • New York City
  • New Jersey Park
  • Luoyang, China
  • Miami, Florida

To inquire about availability, special requests, and/or a detailed estimate, please request a consultation.
